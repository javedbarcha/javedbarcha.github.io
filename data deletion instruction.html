Data Deletion Instructions

For Candy Brustella

If you want to delete your data associated with the game "Candy Brustella", please follow the instructions below.

Important: Candy Brustella uses Facebook Login for authentication. To delete your data from our servers, you must initiate the process through Facebook's official mechanism.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Visit Your Facebook Settings
    Go to your Facebook Account's "Settings & Privacy" section. Click on "Settings".
  2. Navigate to Apps and Websites
    On the left menu, click "Apps and Websites". You will see a list of all the apps and websites you've used Facebook to log in to.
  3. Find Candy Brustella
    Find and click on "Candy Brustella" in the list.
  4. Remove the App
    Click the "Remove" button.
  5. Request Data Deletion (This is the crucial step)
    After clicking "Remove," a pop-up will appear with options.
    You must select the option that says:
    "Also delete all posts, photos and other activity that Candy Brustella may have published on your behalf." OR "Delete your activity and data from Candy Brustella."
    You must check this box to notify us that you want your data deleted from our game's database.

What Happens Next?

Once you complete the steps above, Facebook will send a secure notification to our server confirming your deletion request. Our team will then process this request and permanently delete all your associated game data from our systems. This includes your user profile, game progress, and any other information linked to your account.

This process is usually completed within 30 days.

Please Note: Simply uninstalling the game from your device will not automatically delete your data from our servers. You must follow the steps above to formally request deletion.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or encounter any problems while trying to delete your data, please contact our support team for assistance. We're here to help.
